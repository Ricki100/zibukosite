WHY CHOOSE US?

Zibuko was founded with a vision to fulfill the need for a more meaningful model to provide efficient financing to Zimbabweans in an environment where financial inclusion is low and barriers to traditional lenders are high.

We observed that everyday, people needed short term assistance with their finance to deal with both expected and unexpected expenses or to acquire specific assets but were unable to efficiently access finance from traditional lenders. We understood the needs and the pressures faced by those who are cash and credit contstrained. Zibuko Capital was formed with this in mind. Zibuko Capital is a new, flexible solution to bridging finance manged by a young and dynamic team with financial services experience
